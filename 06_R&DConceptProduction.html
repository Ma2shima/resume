Milano Design Week 2018 Hidden Senses Exhibition

Period: 2016 - 2018


Project Overview

Pursued "comfort" in future living, proposing new ways for information and technology to blend into daily life.


Responsibilities

Interaction design, concretization of the exhibition concept.


Achievements

Received the MILANO DESIGN AWARD BEST PLAYFULNESS. Attracted 386,000 visitors from 65 countries, garnered over 1,000 media mentions, and recorded over 100,000 social media shares.


Staff: Creative Director: Tayuki, Art Directors: Akagawa / Shiono, ID Designers: Yokota / Ogino / Kuwao, UX Designers: Oki / Nakajima / Matsushima, Sound Designer: Nagahara, Communication Designers: Richard / Kaneda, Space Designers: Kikuya / Mitsugi, Coordinators: Kubota / Nagakura, Promoters: Yamauchi / Watanabe / Endo / Iwata, Engineers: Shigeta / Komori / Takahashi / Shimizu / Matsuyama
Tools: Adobe AfterEffects / TouchDesigner / Python / Arduino
