Gaming Rehabilitation Program

Period: 2020 - 2024


Project Overview

Developed a physical therapy program using controller-free video games for elderly residents in long-term care facilities, in partnership with Sony Life Care Group.
Friendly Competition in a Senior Living Community



Responsibilities

UX design and game direction for rehabilitation games.


Achievements

Received the IAUD International Design Award 2023 Gold Award. Planned and conducted an internal hackathon, generating over 200 new rehabilitation game ideas. 12 were prototyped, and 2 underwent Proof of Concept (PoC) for commercialization.
Wellness Games for Everyone The Sony Group
